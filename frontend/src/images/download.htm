Presidential Suite

Fit for A-list actors and Fortune 500 CEOs, this 2,000 ft² (609.6 m²) suite offers awe-inspiring views of Lake Michigan, the Chicago River and the Chicago skyline. Elegant artwork and greenery are placed throughout the space along with a working fireplace and full bar, giving the suite a sophisticated yet personalized touch.

Key Features

  • Lake, river and skyline views
  • Spacious parlor
  • Two baths
  • Dining area
  • Wet bar
  • Oversized executive workspace
  • 2,000 ft² (609.6 m²)
  • Coffee-making facilities
  • 50” flat panel TV

Common Amenities

  • WiFi and broadband internet
  • LCD split-screen TV
  • Separate bath & shower
  • In-room safe
  • Hair dryer
  • Onsite dry-cleaning services
